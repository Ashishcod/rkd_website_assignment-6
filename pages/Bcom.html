Why B.Com ?

This course is designed to provide students with wide range of managerial skills and building competence in a particular area of business studies. Students are exposed to general business principles like Accounting, Human resources, Banking, Statistics, Economics etc. The interdisciplinary nature of program makes them ready to face the challenging business problems. The major and minor subjects helps them to choose subjects according to their skill and likings of these subjects.

Scope:

  • Can pursue higher studies like M.Com, M.B.A , M.HRD, C.A,C.S.
  • Apply for jobs in any government and Banking sector.
  • It also helps who has their own business.
